What?

Comfortable Reader is an intentionally simple, quick, and good looking client app for Instapaper.

It downloads the 20 most recently added bookmarks from your Instapaper unread items folder and makes them available online and offline in a mobile-friendly reading view.

Designed to be ready whenever you have an idle moment to do some reading.

Important You must have a paid subscription to Instapaper in order to use Comfortable Reader.

Download for Windows Phone

Features

  • Full articles and images cached for offline reading
  • Tracks your progress and gets you back quick smart
  • Archive, delete, and favourite articles
  • Tracks and synchs your reading progress
  • Everythings works offline and synchs next time you connect
  • Background synch agent when the app isn't running
  • Add links to Instapaper and share
  • Smells of rich mahogany
