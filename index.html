Comfortable Reader

The snazziest Instapaper client for Windows Phone

Coming Soon. E-mail support@comfortablereader.com with details of your phone to get access to the beta app.

Comfortable Reader is an deliberately simple client app for Instapaper.

It downloads up to 20 most recently added bookmarks from your Instapaper unread items folder and makes them available in a mobile-friendly reading view.

Important You must have a paid subscription to Instapaper in order to use Comfortable Reader. Instapaper allows apps to add bookmarks for free, but to download and read bookmarks from an app requires you to subscribe which is a very reasonable $1 per month.

  • Full articles and images cached for offline reading
  • Tracks your progress and gets you back quick smart
  • Archive, delete, and favourite articles
  • Tracks and synchs your reading progress
  • Everythings works offline and synchs next time you connect
  • Background synch agent when the app isn't running
  • Add links to Instapaper and share
  • Smells of rich mahogany
