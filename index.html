Comfortable Reader

The snazziest Instapaper client for Windows Phone

What?

Comfortable Reader is an intentionally simple, quick, and good looking client app for Instapaper.

It downloads the 20 most recently added bookmarks from your Instapaper unread items folder and makes them available online and offline in a mobile-friendly reading view.

Designed to be ready whenever you have an idle moment to do some reading.

Important You must have a paid subscription to Instapaper in order to use Comfortable Reader.

Download for Windows Phone

Features

  • Remembers what you were reading and gets you back there super-quick
  • Full articles and images cached for offline reading
  • Great looking animated Metro-style article list
  • Archive, delete, and favourite articles
  • Share links using email or socail networks
  • Animated live tile for your home screen
  • Navigate through articles using page up and page down taps or drag and flick gestures
  • Tracks and synchs your reading progress
  • Choice of typeface, text size, and reading background colour
  • All actions work offline and are synchronised next time you're connected
  • Follow links within articles and add them to Instapaper
  • Background agent to synchronise and cache content even when the app isn't running

Why?

There are already some good Instapaper clients for Windows Phone but none of them were the app I was looking for. I wanted something that is:

  • Better looking than just a plain old text list
  • Quick and simple to synchronise and download enough things to read to keep me occupied while waiting for the bus without having to wait for hundreds of things to update.
  • Able to handle interruptions and quickly get me back to what I was reading

So I made Comfortable Reader with this in mind. It deliberately excludes folder management and organisation to help keep the app as simple to use and quick as possible.

I hope you like it!
